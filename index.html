Who We Are

About Dr. Sarabhai IAS Academy

Established in Mavelikara, Dr. Sarabhai IAS Academy is dedicated to nurturing future leaders through quality education and expert coaching for competitive exams. Inaugurated by Padmavibhushan Dr. G. Madhavan Nair (ISRO Chairman, 2018), the academy is led by Dr. Mohanan M.P., Senior Scientist (BARC, Govt. of India), PhD Guide & Consultant.

  • Coaching for IAS, KAS, PSC, SSC, Banking, NEET, CBSE, ICSE, and more.
  • Integrated syllabus and scholarship exams for school students (Class 5 & above).
  • Online & offline classes, school bus facility, and vacation batches available.
